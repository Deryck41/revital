Declaration and obligations

Step 5/6
01
I vouch that all the information’s given above are true and I authorize the Owner and/or his representative to obtain or exchange personal information to research agencies to help establish or check my financial situation. My deposit is applicable on my first month’s rent, if my application request is accepted. However, if I change my mind after filling out and signing this form, I renounce the reimbursement of my deposit. In fact, I hereby understand that the present signed application attests that I am formally undertaking to lease the dwelling in the event that the Landlord approve my application and once my offer is accepted by the landlord. I undertake to sign the lease documents submitted to me by the landlord.
02
Upon approval from the landlord, the applicant (s) agrees to assume full responsibility for the premises and all obligations outlined in the forthcoming lease agreement. This includes, but is not limited to, the payment of rent, maintenance of the premises, adherence to property rules and regulations, and any other terms and conditions stipulated in the lease.
03
The applicant(s) acknowledges that this Promise to Lease becomes a binding agreement upon the landlord's approval. The applicant's responsibilities and obligations under this agreement will be effective from the moment the Landlord expresses his approval.
04
In the event that no lease is signed for the dwelling through the applicant’s fault, the applicant undertakes to compensate directly the agency or the broker, by paying damages equal to the renumeration that the Landlord would otherwise have had to pay. Should the applicant refuse to pay the damages, the Landlord reserves the right to institute legal proceedings.
05
The present is gouverned by the laws of Quebec.
By signing this Promise to Lease, the applicant(s) confirms their understanding and acceptance of these terms and agrees to fulfill all obligations as specified.
